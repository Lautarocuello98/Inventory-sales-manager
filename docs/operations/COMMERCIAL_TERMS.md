 Commercial Terms (template)

> This repository keeps MIT license for source code reference. For commercial distribution to end customers, use a separate commercial agreement/EULA.

## Recommended clauses
- License scope: per-seat or per-site.
- Maintenance term and renewal.
- Support scope and SLA reference.
- Limitation of liability.
- Backup responsibility and retention policy.
- Data privacy and incident communication window.

## Action required before selling
- Publish signed customer-facing EULA.
- Add billing, invoicing, and support contacts.
- Version and archive all customer contracts.
